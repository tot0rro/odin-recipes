Arroz con Leche

Description

Today we are learning how to make arroz con leche. Above is an image of the finished product, it is mexican rice pudding made with rice, milk and a few extra sweet ingredients. There is a variety of ways to make this sweet dessert. Here is my way, let's begin.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup of uncooked white rice
  • 2 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cinnamon stick

Steps

  1. Clean rice until water runs clear.
  2. Combine water and rice in a bowl. Let soak, at least 30 minutes.
  3. Bring water and rice to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and liquid has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. Stir milk, sugar, salt, and cinnamon stick into the saucepan. Simmer until thick and the consistency of pudding, about 20 minutes. Discard cinnamon stick before serving.
